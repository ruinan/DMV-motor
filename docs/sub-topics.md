## Per-Sub-Topic Description

### 1. `TRAFFIC_REGULATORY_SIGNS` — Regulatory Signs

What it tests: recognition and response to regulatory signs — stop signs, yield signs, posted speed limits, no-turn restrictions, one-way signs. Includes the "stop-then-edge-forward-then-stop-again" procedure at blind intersections from the motorcycle handbook.

### 2. `TRAFFIC_LANE_MARKINGS` — Lane Markings

What it tests: meaning of solid/dashed white and yellow lines, two-way left-turn center lanes, HOV markings, crosswalks, stop bars, parking restrictions (curb colors). Mostly base driver handbook material; motorcycle handbook adds parking-angle rules (45-90 degrees, wheel touching curb).

### 3. `ROW_INTERSECTIONS` — Right of Way at Intersections

What it tests: who goes first at four-way stops, uncontrolled intersections, T-intersections, left turns against oncoming traffic. The motorcycle handbook calls out that **half of motorcycle-vehicle collisions** happen when a driver enters a rider's right-of-way — this is the highest-risk situation for new riders.

### 4. `ROW_VULNERABLE_USERS` — Vulnerable Users

What it tests: yielding to pedestrians at crosswalks, sharing the road with bicyclists, responding to emergency vehicles (sirens/lights). Plus the motorcycle-specific hazard of passing parked vehicles (door zone, pedestrian-from-between).

### 5. `SPEED_FOLLOWING_DISTANCE` — Following Distance

What it tests: the 2-second following rule (extending in adverse conditions), being followed too closely (move over / change lanes / let them pass), reading the gap between you and the vehicle ahead.

### 6. `SPEED_LIMITS_PASSING` — Speed Limits & Passing

What it tests: posted vs basic speed law, when passing is legal, completing a pass quickly and returning to your lane, being passed (don't speed up), merging onto freeways.

### 7. `LANE_POSITION_SELECTION` — Three-Position Lane

What it tests: choosing left / center / right portion of the lane based on traffic flow, visibility, road conditions, and surface hazards. A core motorcycle-only skill not present in passenger-vehicle exams.

### 8. `LANE_SPLITTING_SHARING` — Lane Splitting

What it tests: California's lane-splitting rules (legal but requires caution), reasonable speed differentials, not splitting near intersections or large vehicles, lane sharing between motorcycles.

### 9. `TURNING_CURVES` — Cornering

What it tests: identifying constant-radius vs decreasing-radius vs widening curves, slowing before entry vs accelerating through, lane position through a curve (outside-inside-outside line), countersteering basics.

### 10. `MANEUVERS_SWERVE_BRAKE` — Swerving & Emergency Braking

What it tests: separating swerve and brake (never both at once, especially with front brake), choosing swerve-then-brake vs brake-then-swerve based on space, using both brakes in a straight line, threshold braking on the front.

### 11. `ALCOHOL_BAC_LAW` — BAC & DUI Law

What it tests: California BAC limits (0.08% adult, 0.04% commercial, 0.01% under-21), DUI penalties (license suspension, fines, jail), implied consent, zero-tolerance for minors.

### 12. `DRUGS_IMPAIRMENT` — Drug Impairment & Risk Reduction

What it tests: drug impairment beyond alcohol (prescription, OTC, cannabis), the "minimize the risks" guidance (don't drink, designate a rider, take a cab), the unique vulnerability of motorcyclists to even small amounts of impairment.

### 13. `SURFACES_SLIPPERY_TRACKS` — Surface Hazards

What it tests: handling wet roads / leaves / gravel / metal grates / painted lines / oil spots, crossing railroad and trolley tracks at as close to 90° as practical, riding over pavement seams.

### 14. `EMERGENCIES_MECHANICAL` — Mechanical Emergencies

What it tests: responding to tire failure (don't grab brake on flat), stuck throttle (clutch + kill switch), chain breakage, engine seizure, safely getting off the road (signal, hand-on-clutch coast to shoulder).

### 15. `BASICS_PPE` — Protective Gear

What it tests: U.S. DOT helmet requirement and how to verify it, helmet types (full / modular / 3/4 / half) and trade-offs, face/eye protection (face shield > goggles > glasses), proper clothing (jacket / long pants / over-the-ankle boots / gloves).

### 16. `BASICS_CONTROL_INSPECTION` — Pre-Ride & Controls

What it tests: pre-trip inspection checklist (tires, controls, lights, oil, chassis, stand), motorcycle controls identification (clutch, throttle, brakes, gear shift), body position (arms relaxed, knees against tank, feet on pegs), basic braking (both brakes, smooth not abrupt).
